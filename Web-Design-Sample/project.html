Project

A project is a planned and coordinated effort to achieve a specific goal or objective within a defined timeframe, utilizing a set of resources and a specific approach or methodology. It typically involves a team of individuals working together, with specific roles and responsibilities, to complete a series of tasks and deliverables. Projects can vary in complexity and scope, from small and straightforward endeavors to large-scale, complex initiatives that involve multiple stakeholders, extensive planning, and ongoing management. Examples of projects could include building a new software application, constructing a new building, launching a marketing campaign, or conducting a research study.
